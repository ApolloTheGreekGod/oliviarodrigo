Bio


Olivia Rodrigo is a 18 year old singer-songwriter,
and writing songs is her favourite thing to do. She
started writing her first song when she was only
a child and she started singing lessons early in her
childhood. Her first official song ever released was
was called “All I Want.” She wrote it for a Disney
show called 'High School Musical: The Musical :The
series'. Fast forwarding towards the last day before
Covid lockdown, she met Interscope/Geffen records
and was introduced to a producer named Dan Nigro
for the first time. They proceeded to develop the
hit song 'drivers license' and hit album 'SOUR'.
